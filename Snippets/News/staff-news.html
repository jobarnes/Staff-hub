Matrix CMS upgrade – content freeze 14-30 April

The University’s Matrix Content Management System (CMS) will be locked to editors and site administrators as a major upgrade is undertaken from Monday 14 April to Wednesday 30 April. CMS site administrators are requested to plan ahead and make necessary changes. The content freeze is required to ensure the system can be updated with little interference.

Procedures for emergency changes will be available if absolutely required.
